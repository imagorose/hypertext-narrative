The feeling of the growing full moon becomes too much to bear, and you reach for their knife as a last resort to stop the oncoming metamorphosis. They recoil, a quizzical look across their face, as if expecting you to say something. But the words do not come out, and they instead turn into the sound of animalistic jaws snapping at the air. The arm you extended retracts fearfully, not wanting them to know the truth of your biology. Taking hesitant steps backward, your werewolf form is frighteningly clear to them.

But, rather than flee, they approach you cautiously, bringing their hands to yours.
