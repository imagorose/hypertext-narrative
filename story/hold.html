In a desperate effort to convey your affection for them, you reach for their hand. You are much too late. The full moon taunts you, egging on your grotesque transformation. The claws of your hands grow sharper and jagged, coarse hair covers your body. Your once human face elongates into a snarling maw, fangs bared and at the ready. You didn't want them to see you this way, a monstrous creation of the supernatural.

You loved this person, and they you, but now, they cowered in fear, grasping for the weapon at their side.
